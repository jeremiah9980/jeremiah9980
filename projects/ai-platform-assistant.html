AI-Assisted Platform Engineering Workflow

A practical, enterprise-safe workflow for accelerating platform architecture, documentation, and reference implementation delivery using AI — while keeping final technical judgment, validation, and ownership human-led.

What This Project Demonstrates

Platform engineering often requires producing high-quality architecture, documentation, and implementation scaffolding quickly — across multiple domains (infrastructure, CI/CD, Kubernetes, SRE, observability, networking).

This project captures an AI-assisted workflow where AI is treated as an accelerator: it drafts and normalizes artifacts, while I define constraints, review outputs, validate correctness, and publish sanitized deliverables suitable for public portfolios.

Workflow Architecture

  • Requirements & Constraints — Scope, risk boundaries, and acceptance criteria are defined upfront.
  • Draft Generation — AI produces structured drafts (pages, READMEs, diagrams, repo scaffolds).
  • Human Validation — Technical correctness, safety, and narrative quality are reviewed and refined.
  • Sanitization — Removes proprietary details; keeps patterns, decisions, and operational thinking.
  • Publish & Iterate — Versioned commits to GitHub; feedback drives improvements.

Inputs

  • Clear goals, constraints, and desired outcomes (enterprise audience)
  • Domain expertise across infrastructure, cloud, Kubernetes, CI/CD, SRE
  • Risk posture: avoid proprietary systems, sensitive configs, customer data
  • Quality bar: Staff/Principal tone, design-review-level narrative

Outputs

This workflow produces public, sanitized artifacts that mirror real platform engineering work:

  • GitHub Pages portfolio (Projects, Resume, About, Contact)
  • Project deep-dive pages with consistent structure and leadership tone
  • Reference repositories (Terraform, Kubernetes, CI/CD, SRE, Networking)
  • Sanitized architecture diagrams used across project pages and READMEs
  • Reusable templates (runbooks, SLO guidance, postmortems, checklists)

Engineering Practices Emphasized

Design Reviews Operational Readiness Risk Management Documentation at Scale Reference Architectures Sanitization & Safety Iteration & Versioning

Why This Matters at Senior Levels

At Staff/Principal levels, impact comes from leverage: producing clarity, accelerating delivery, and improving reliability without increasing risk. This workflow shows how AI can be integrated into engineering operations as a controlled accelerator — not a replacement for judgment.
