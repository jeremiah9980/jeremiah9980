AI Platform Assistant

A human-led, AI-assisted platform engineering system that accelerates infrastructure analysis, deployment reviews, and operational decision-making across cloud, Kubernetes, CI/CD, and SRE domains.

AI + Infrastructure Platform Engineering Read-Only Policy-Aware Enterprise-Safe

What this is

Platform teams rely on dispersed signals (Terraform state, Kubernetes metadata, CI/CD pipelines, SLOs, alerts, runbooks). This project shows how to unify those signals into a safe, advisory AI layer that improves speed and clarity without bypassing change control.

Architecture

Core capabilities

  • Infrastructure analysis: summarize risk, drift, and dependencies using read-only platform metadata.
  • Deployment readiness: review CI/CD pipelines for gates, rollbacks, and safe rollout patterns.
  • Incident assistance: guide responders using observability context (SLIs/SLOs, alerts, timelines).
  • Guardrails: policy-aware outputs, no destructive actions, human-in-the-loop validation.

Safety & guardrails

  • No destructive actions (advisory only)
  • Read-only ingestion of platform metadata
  • Human approval required for changes
  • No secrets, credentials, customer data, or employer-confidential detail
