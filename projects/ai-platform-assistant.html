AI Platform Assistant

A human-led, AI-assisted platform engineering system that accelerates infrastructure analysis, deployment review, and operational decision-making across cloud, Kubernetes, CI/CD, and SRE domains.

AI + Infrastructure Platform Engineering Read-Only Policy-Aware Enterprise-Safe

Problem Context

Modern infrastructure teams operate complex platforms spanning cloud, Kubernetes, CI/CD pipelines, and observability systems. Critical knowledge is often fragmented across dashboards, configs, and runbooks — slowing decision-making during deployments and incidents.

This project demonstrates how AI can be applied safely to infrastructure environments as an **advisory system**, providing context-aware guidance while preserving human ownership, change control, and risk management.

Architecture Overview

Core Capabilities

  • Infrastructure Analysis — Reviews cloud, Terraform, and Kubernetes metadata to identify risks and inconsistencies
  • Deployment Review — Evaluates CI/CD pipelines for readiness, safety gates, and rollout risk
  • Incident Assistance — Guides responders through diagnostics using observability and SLO context
  • Policy & Guardrails — Enforces read-only access, human-in-the-loop validation, and auditability

Safety & Design Principles

  • No destructive actions — advisory only
  • Read-only ingestion of platform metadata
  • Human validation required for all decisions
  • No secrets, credentials, or customer data
  • Fully sanitized reference architecture
