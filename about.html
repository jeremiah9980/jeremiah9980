About Me

I’m a senior infrastructure and platform engineer with over two decades of hands-on experience designing, automating, and operating enterprise-scale systems across on-prem, cloud, and hybrid environments.

Professional Background

My career spans the full infrastructure lifecycle — from traditional data center environments and virtualization platforms, through modern cloud-native architectures, Kubernetes platforms, CI/CD systems, and Site Reliability Engineering practices.

I specialize in building platforms that are:

  • Reliable by design, not by heroics
  • Automated, observable, and operable at scale
  • Secure, segmented, and auditable
  • Aligned with real business and engineering workflows

I’m most effective at the intersection of architecture, automation, and operations — where systems must not only work, but continue to work under growth, change, and failure.

My work with CosmicGen centers on:

This work reflects real-world challenges around capacity planning, multi-tenant isolation, reliability, and rapid iteration in early-stage environments.

Engineering Philosophy

I believe strong platforms are built on clarity — clear ownership, clear interfaces, and clear operational expectations.

I value:

  • Designing for failure and recovery, not just success
  • Automating repeatable work and documenting the rest
  • Blameless learning over reactive firefighting
  • Systems that empower teams rather than constrain them

The projects on this site are intentionally sanitized and production-inspired. They demonstrate architecture, tradeoffs, and operational thinking without exposing proprietary systems or data.
